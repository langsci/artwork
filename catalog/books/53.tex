\bblurb{This book presents groundbreaking robotic experiments on how and why spatial language evolves.
It provides detailed explanations of the origins of spatial conceptualization strategies, spatial categories, landmark systems and spatial grammar by tracing the interplay of environmental conditions, communicative and cognitive pressures. }
